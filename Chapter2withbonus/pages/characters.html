Characters

Thor: The God of Thunder, who is on a journey of self-discovery and emotional healing after losing many loved ones. He’s determined to protect the universe and deal with his past, including his relationship with Jane Foster.
Jane Foster / The Mighty Thor: Thor’s ex-girlfriend, who is diagnosed with cancer. She finds Mjolnir and becomes the Mighty Thor, taking on the mantle of a hero to help save the day, despite her declining health.
Gorr the God Butcher: The main antagonist of the film. Gorr is a deeply wounded and vengeful character who seeks to kill all gods after losing his daughter and being rejected by the gods.
Valkyrie: The new King of New Asgard. She is a fierce warrior who helps Thor and his friends in their fight against Gorr and other threats to Asgard.
Korg: A rock-like alien and Thor's loyal companion. He provides comic relief and acts as the film's narrator, reflecting on the events of the story.
Star-Lord: The leader of the Guardians of the Galaxy, who briefly joins forces with Thor at the start of the film. He has a humorous, yet emotionally vulnerable relationship with his teammates.
Rocket: A member of the Guardians of the Galaxy, known for his sharp wit and skill in combat. He helps Thor and the team with various battles.
Drax: A member of the Guardians of the Galaxy, who is a literal and somewhat clueless character, offering humor in his interactions.
Mantis: Another member of the Guardians of the Galaxy, who has empathic abilities and is a kind-hearted, quirky character.
Nebula: A former villain turned ally of the Guardians, Nebula is a warrior with a tough exterior and a complex relationship with her sister Gamora.
Zeus: The king of the Greek gods, who proves to be a morally questionable and self-indulgent figure, unwilling to help Thor against Gorr’s threat.
Lady Sif: A fierce warrior and former ally of Thor. She’s gravely wounded in battle with Gorr's forces and warns Thor about the god butcher.
Axl: The son of Heimdall, who plays a crucial role in guiding Thor and his team to the captured Asgardian children.
Heimdall: The all-seeing Asgardian guardian, who appears in a post-credits scene to welcome Jane Foster into Valhalla.
Rapu: The god who rejects Gorr's plea for help, leading to Gorr’s vow to kill all gods.
Gorr's Minions (Black Berserkers): Gorr’s monstrous minions who help him in his quest to hunt down and destroy the gods.
Love: Gorr's daughter, whom Thor adopts after Gorr’s death. She becomes Thor’s new companion, with Thor raising her after the tragic events of the film.
